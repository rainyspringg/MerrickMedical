Our Services

Merrick Medical Center has been located at 122, Doncaster Rd, Doncaster for 20 years. We are a friendly General Practice and thrive on putting the patients first – with our motto being “caring for your wellness”. The practice prioritises the patient’s needs and we want to provide a comprehensive service for the local and wider community. We are an accredited practice and we aim to provide our patients with the highest possible quality of care with the most currents treatments, methods, materials and equipment within our resource capabilities. We welcome new patients and we accommodate patients with urgent, non-urgent, complex, chronic and preventative health needs.
