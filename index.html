Merrick Medical Center

Merrick Medical Center is a multi-disciplinary medical centre committed to quality family medical care. The practice is fully accredited, with all the doctors vocationally registered to ensure a high standard of medical care.

Billing

Please note that our clinic is MIXED BILLING – we guarantee our patients who have a Health Care Card, Pensioner Card, or who are under 16 years of age, a bulk-billed consultation, given that they also have a valid Medicare care. Patients without these concessions may be bulk-billed at the discretion of the doctor their appointment is with.
